Scavenger Hunt

Welcome to the scavenger hunt page!

You'll be shown a series of clues that will guide you to different locations around the museum. Each clue will describe a location where a QR code has been hidden. Scan the QR code with your phone to be shown the next clue. Your progress will be tracked on your account at the bottom of the page.

Some basic rules:

  • Don't move or hide the clues from others
  • The clues are all hidden in publicly accessible areas (none are in bathrooms).
  • Try not to spoil the clue locations for others
  • Feel free to work with others!
